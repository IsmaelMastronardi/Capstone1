The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activist who comprise the Creative Commons affiliate networks in a different country every two. This year, we hope to expand our invitations list including organizations and individuals

Experience the magic of classical music at the Cordoba Spring Festival, as we celebrate the brilliance of Beethoven, Mozart, and Handel. From powerful symphonies to exquisite concertos, immerse yourself in the enchanting world of these iconic composers.
